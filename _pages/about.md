<span style="font-size:24px;">Welcome to SANSL!</span>

<span style="font-size:17px;">
SANSL is a research group studying system security and network security, led by Prof. Minkyung Park. 
We aim to protect user privacy by identifying vulnerabilities and developing secure designs. We leverage techniques such as dynamic information flow control, fuzzing, PKI/TLS, and trusted execution environments such as Intel SGX and Arm TrustZone. 
<a href="/research/">Learn more about our research here.</a>

We are currently looking for motivated students to join our lab. If you are interested, please feel free to contact us by email.
