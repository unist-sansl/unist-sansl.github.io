We study system and network security to protect user privacy by identifying vulnerabilities and developing secure designs. Our research covers dynamic information flow control, fuzzing, PKI/TLS, and trusted execution environments such as Intel SGX and Arm TrustZone.

We are currently looking for motivated students to join our lab, and interested students are encouraged to email us.
